    How to test component?

    Treat your component as a black box:

    • props
    • events (onClick, onSelect...)
    • lifecycle events (mounted, destroyed...)
    • event emitted
    • output (DOM)
    • props pass to subcomponents
    • Internal state (data, computed properties)
